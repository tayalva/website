Reminders

iPhone X

This is a simple proximity reminders app that allows you to make reminders based on specific locations. Similar to Apple's built in reminders, you can set entering and/or exiting parameters!

Movie Night

iPhone X

Movie Night is a project designed around the moviedb API. It helps two friends decide what movie to watch by selecting preferences for each, and then displaying a list of movie suggestions for their movie marathon!

Nasa

iPad

Nasa is a simple app designed around NASA's API. Each day you'll get NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day, along with the ability to pull the most recent Mars Rover's Images and create post cards for your friends! You can also search for any location in the world and receive the most recent satellite image of that location.
